I built the BLISTER Score app to bring a published, externally-validated infection-risk model to the bedside — helping target the TYRX antimicrobial envelope to the patients most likely to benefit, where it is most cost-effective.

CIED infection is a serious, costly complication of pacemaker, ICD and CRT procedures, and its incidence is rising. The TYRX antimicrobial envelope reduces infection in high-risk patients (WRAP-IT trial) but is too costly for routine use — so a way to target it is needed.

The BLISTER score (Maclean et al, 2024) was derived from 7,383 procedures at St Bartholomew's Hospital and externally validated in standard- and high-risk cohorts. It extends the older PADIT score and out-performs it (AUC 0.82 vs 0.71). A score of ≥6 is the threshold at which fitting an envelope is cost-effective within NICE willingness-to-pay thresholds: ~30% relative reduction in infection, a number needed to treat of 31, and £18,446 per QALY.

v1.2.0 · June 2026
  • Copy note — generate a paste-ready pre-procedure summary (BLISTER score, factor breakdown and the envelope recommendation) for the SOLUS record. Below the threshold it flags when a prolonged pocket-open time (≥120 min) would tip the score to ≥6.
  • Small-screen layout rebuilt as an app-shell — the domains scroll between a fixed header and a static result panel docked above the footer (no more floating bar).
  • The rainbow spine flourish now replays as a gentle attract loop while the form is untouched, and stops as soon as a factor is selected.
  • Tidier verdict — a real 0-point selection (e.g. a PPM box change) now reads “No envelope by score”, the warranted box shows two key figures, and the eyebrow reads “Envelope warranted”.
v1.1.0 · June 2026
  • New animated launch screen — a pacemaker is wrapped in the TYRX antimicrobial envelope and seated into the device pocket, an antimicrobial force field repels infection, and a “Go to Calculator” reveal lifts the curtain with a muted rainbow flourish down the BLISTER spine.
  • Calculator rebuilt — answer factors sit below each domain and line up on a grid; all are checkboxes, with mutually-exclusive options (age band, device box-change type, previous procedures) handled automatically.
  • Type of procedure now supports combinations — a box change, a new / revised lead and a lead extraction can be scored together.
v1.0.0 · June 2026
  • Initial release — full BLISTER calculator across all seven domains with live scoring.
  • Antimicrobial-envelope verdict at the ≥6 threshold, with projected infection reduction, NNT and cost per QALY.
